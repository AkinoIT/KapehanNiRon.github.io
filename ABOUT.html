Welcome to KAPEHAN NI RON, where passion for coffee meets a cozy ambiance. Established in 2023, we are more than just a coffee shop; we are a community of coffee enthusiasts dedicated to providing a delightful and memorable experience.

OUR STORY
Driven by a love for exceptional coffee, we embarked on a journey to create a haven for caffeine connoisseurs. Our story began by dreaming to own my coffee shop. I started this business, because of the love for coffee and the other coffee shop owners that inspire me to have my own coffee shop.

OUR MISSION
At KAPEHAN NI RON, our mission is to craft the perfect cup of coffee that not only tantalizes your taste buds but also reflects our commitment to quality and sustainability. We strive to create a warm and inviting space where every sip brings joy.

OUR COFFEE
We source the finest beans from around the world, carefully selecting each origin for its unique flavor profile. From the roasting process to the final pour, our baristas are dedicated to ensuring that every cup is a masterpiece.

OUR SPACE
Step into KAPEHAN NI RON and experience more than just a coffee break. Our cozy ambiance is designed to be an extension of your living room - a place to unwind, connect, and savor the moment.

Thank you for choosing us as your coffee destination. We look forward to serving you a cup of happiness soon.
